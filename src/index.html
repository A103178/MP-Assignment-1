My Top 5 Disney Movies

The Good Dinosaur Zootopia Encanto Dumbo Finding Nemo

The Good Dinosaur

Director | Peter Sohn
"The Good Dinosaur" follows a timid young dinosaur named Arlo as he befriends a human boy in a world where dinosaurs never went extinct, learning valuable lessons about courage and friendship.
Released | 2015

Zootopia

Director | Byron Howard, Rich Moore
In "Zootopia," a determined bunny named Judy Hopps teams up with a sly fox, Nick Wilde, to uncover a conspiracy in the bustling city of Zootopia, challenging stereotypes and promoting inclusivity along the way.
Released | 2016

Encanto

Director | Byron Howard,Jared Bush
"Encanto" tells the story of a magical Colombian family member, Mirabel, who discovers her unique abilities and embarks on a journey to save her enchanted home, exploring themes of self-discovery and family bonds.
Released | 2021

Dumbo

Director | Ben Sharpsteen, Bill Roberts, Wilfred Jackson, Norm Ferguson, Samuel Armstrong, Jack Kinney, John Elliotte, and Rich Moore
"Dumbo" narrates the heartwarming tale of a lovable elephant with oversized ears who, initially an outcast, learns to embrace his differences and ultimately soars to success as a star of the circus.
Released | 1941

Finding Nemo

Director | Andrew Stanton
"Finding Nemo" follows the adventurous journey of a clownfish named Marlin as he crosses the ocean to rescue his captured son, Nemo, encountering various marine characters and imparting lessons about family bonds and letting go along the way.
Released | 2003
